About Us

Convenience of Downtown, Comfort of Home

Downtown Dental is a hometown dental practice located in the heart of Greenville, SC specializing in comprehensive family, implant and sedation dentistry. Our practice emphasizes a preventative, total-health approach to help you lead more a healthy, productive life.

Our Total-Health Approach

We build trust by being honest, friendly and compassionate to our patients. We take time to get to know you, and listen carefully to your needs. It is a privilege and responsibility to convey to our patients how dental health is related to our overall physical and emotional well-being. We take the time to educate you from this perspective so you can confidently make decisions regarding your health.
