Our Services

Services Unlike Any Other

Dr. Kenna and the team at Downtown Dental are prepared to take care of you and your oral health. Our focus in on prevention in order to maintain your wellbeing. When treatment is necessary, we are trained in a variety of areas. Below is an overview of some of the services we provide for you.

  • All inclusive exams
  • Oral cancer screenings
  • Digital x-rays
  • Child and adult cleanings
  • Scaling and root planing
  • Sealants
  • Fluoride Treatment
  • Bonding & tooth colored fillings
  • Veneers
  • Crowns & Bridges
  • Implants
  • Extractions
  • Bone grafting after extractions
  • Whitening (trays, strips)
  • Root canal therapy
  • Night guards & Splints
  • Nitrous-oxide gas to reduce anxiety
  • IV Sedation
