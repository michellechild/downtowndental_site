Financial

At Downtown Dental, we understand that finances can play a major role in a patientâ€™s decision to get dental work completed. To assist with the financial aspect of comprehensive oral care, Downtown Dental provides different financial options to their patients.

Insurance

Borrowed from Falls Park DentistryDowntown Dental accepts most dental insurances. Our office is not a Medicaid provider. Most plans are intended to assist with the cost of treatment and do not cover treatment 100%. As a courtesy we will estimate an expected insurance payment. Since it is impossible for the office to guarantee insurance payments, the patient is responsible for any balance that may not be covered. You will receive a statement in the mail for any balance unpaid by insurance.

Financing Options

Downtown Dental provides no-interest payment plans through CareCredit and Citi Health Card. Please ask us about how these can help you achieve your dental goals.

Guarantee

Downtown Dental offers a 3 year guarantee on crowns and veneers and a 1 year guarantee on fillings on the condition that you keep up with your preventative care at Downtown Dental every 6 months.
