Your First Visit

We’re Happy You’re Here

Borrowed from BrightNow! DentalGoing to the dentist can be an overwhelming experience, especially first-time visits. At Downtown Dental, we make every effort to put you at ease from the moment you enter our door.

Our patients receive:

  • A warm, friendly greeting when you call or stop in.
  • Doctor and staff members who treat you with respect.
  • Quality, affordable dental care for you and your family.
  • A clear explanation of every aspect of your visit.
  • Our sincere appreciation for choosing and recommending us.

What to Bring

To make your visit as pleasant and brief as possible, we ask you download and bring these forms with you. If you have any questions about the forms, we will be happy to answer them at the time of your visit.

What to Expect

The dentist will perform a complete oral examination and discuss any treatment you may need. The examination can identify conditions such as tooth decay, periodontal disease, and even oral cancer.


Teeth cleaning may be performed during your first visit, or might be scheduled for a follow-up visit depending on the condition of your teeth and gums.


Your first appointment will take one to one and a half hours. Patients under the age of 18 must be accompanied by a parent.
